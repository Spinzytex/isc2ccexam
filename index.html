A finance clerk notices that a wire transfer amount in a shared spreadsheet was changed from $10,000 to $100,000 without anyone's authorization, and no one flagged the change. Which security principle has MOST likely been violated?

  • A. Confidentiality
  • B. Integrity
  • C. Availability
  • D. Non-repudiation
Correct Answer: B
AConfidentiality concerns unauthorized disclosure of information, not unauthorized alteration of its content.
BIntegrity ensures data remains accurate and has not been modified without authorization; an unapproved change to the transfer amount is a direct integrity failure.
CAvailability concerns whether authorized users can access data and systems when needed, which is not the issue described here.
DNon-repudiation ensures a party cannot deny having performed an action; the scenario is about an unauthorized change, not about denying responsibility for one.
